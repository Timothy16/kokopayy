KOKO SANS — the Kokosend / Kokopay typeface
============================================
Version 1.000 · finalized 2026-06-01

THE FAMILY (8 styles)
  KokoSans-Regular        (400)        KokoSans-Italic          (400 italic)
  KokoSans-Medium         (500)        KokoSans-MediumItalic    (500 italic)
  KokoSans-SemiBold       (600)        KokoSans-SemiBoldItalic  (600 italic)
  KokoSans-Bold           (700)        KokoSans-BoldItalic      (700 italic)
Each ships as .ttf (install/desktop) and .woff (web). 502 glyphs each.
Note: Medium & SemiBold (and their italics) were interpolated from the
Regular and Bold masters — true intermediate weights, not algorithmic fakes.

WEB USE
  koko-sans.css  — ready @font-face rules. Put the four .woff files in a
  ./fonts/ folder next to the CSS, link the CSS, then use:
      font-family:"Koko Sans";   (font-weight:400/700, font-style:normal/italic)

WHAT WAS DONE
  • Forked from Instrument Sans (SIL Open Font License v1.1).
  • Renamed throughout to "Koko Sans" — the reserved name "Instrument Sans"
    appears nowhere (an OFL requirement for modified versions).
  • Added a Naira sign ₦ (U+20A6) — Instrument Sans had none. Built as the
    font's own N with two crossbars, mapped to U+20A6, in all four styles.
  • Original copyright + the OFL license are retained in each file's
    metadata; Kokosend Limited's modification copyright is recorded.

LEGAL — PLAIN ENGLISH
  • The OFL EXPLICITLY allows modifying, renaming, embedding, redistributing
    and commercial use, for free. This is the opposite of the proprietary-font
    situation that caused the earlier dispute.
  • Your only obligation when you DISTRIBUTE the font files (host on the site,
    bundle in an app): include OFL.txt alongside them. No credit is required
    anywhere in your UI.
  • Keep further modified versions under the OFL; don't use the name
    "Instrument Sans"; don't sell the font file by itself.
  • NOT EXCLUSIVE: legally safe and customisable, but Instrument Sans remains
    available to others. True exclusivity = a paid bespoke commission.
